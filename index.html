    about me

    Hi, I'm Jamie, a passionate Computer Games Programming student. I've built a solid foundation with two years of college, a year of industry experience during that time, and a year at university. All this has really honed my skills and love for game development.

    I'm skilled in C++, C#, and Unity, and I've used these tools in various projects throughout my studies. I love tackling new challenges and solving complex problems in game development.

    I'm always eager to learn new things and enjoy the thrill of overcoming obstacles. The fast-paced world of technology and the creativity needed to create immersive games keep me excited and motivated.

    When I'm not studying or working on projects, I'm usually gaming or creating my own games. I also enjoy watching shows and reading, which often spark ideas for my game projects.

    I'm always excited to connect with others who share my passion for gaming. Feel free to reach out to me via email, LinkedIn, or check out my work on GitHub.
